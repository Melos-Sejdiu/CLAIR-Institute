About CLAIR Institute

CLAIR Institute is a scientific association founded in Prishtina on 22/05/2025, dedicated to advancing research in cognitive psychology, linguistics, and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to address complex scientific and technological challenges through innovative research and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Mission & Objectives

  • Conduct advanced research on human cognitive processes using innovative scientific methods.
  • Develop and implement research projects focused on linguistics, leveraging artificial intelligence for language analysis.
  • Promote the application of AI technologies for natural human-machine interaction and language understanding.
  • Provide a platform for international collaboration on innovative research projects addressing emerging technological challenges.
  • Advance the integration of humanities and technology to address future societal challenges.

Fields of Activity

  • Research & Analysis
  • Information Technology
  • Science
  • Social & Psychological Support
  • Education & Training
